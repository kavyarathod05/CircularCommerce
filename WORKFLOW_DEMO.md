# 🎤 Hackathon Live Demo Workflow
**SecondLife Commerce**

Use this step-by-step workflow to flawlessly demo your application to the judges, highlighting the customer-centric features and the powerful ML backend working together.

---

### Step 1: The Persona Selection
1. **Action:** Open the app and land on the **Role Selection Screen**.
2. **Talking Point:** "Our platform serves a two-sided marketplace. First, let's step into the shoes of an eco-conscious buyer."
3. **Action:** Click **"I am a Buyer"**.

### Step 2: Discovery & Intelligent Conversion
1. **Action:** You land on the **Browse Catalog** tab.
2. **Talking Point:** "Notice how the prices aren't static. Our *Dynamic Pricing Engine* has dropped the price of these headphones by 10% because local inventory is high. For apparel, our *AI Size Recommendation Engine* guarantees the perfect fit before the user even clicks."
3. **Action:** Click **"Add to Cart"** on an item (e.g., the Cotton Hoodie).

### Step 3: Predictive Friction & Intervention
1. **Action:** Navigate to the **Your Cart** tab.
2. **Talking Point:** "Here's where we intercept costly returns *before* they happen. If a user tries to 'bracket' by adding multiple sizes of the same item, or if they have a historically high return rate, our ML model flags the cart as High Risk."
3. **Action:** (Optional) Open the `🛠️ Hackathon Demo Controls` accordion and increase the *Simulate Historical Returns* to `5`. Watch the UI instantly switch to **"⚠️ Fit Uncertainty Detected"**.
4. **Talking Point:** "Instead of just blocking the user, we offer a solution to save carbon emissions."
5. **Action:** Click **"Use AI Virtual Try-On 👗"**.

### Step 4: The Cure (Virtual Try-On)
1. **Action:** The **Virtual Try-On** tab opens.
2. **Talking Point:** "The buyer uploads a quick photo, and our generative AI model drapes the exact garment over their body in seconds. Fit uncertainty is eliminated, they buy with confidence, and a future return is prevented."

### Step 5: The Magic Return (AI Condition Triage)
1. **Action:** Navigate to the **Start a Return** tab.
2. **Talking Point:** "But what if a return *does* happen? Instead of the customer shipping a box across the country to wait 14 days for a refund, we use AI to process it instantly from their living room."
3. **Action:** Select an item from the dropdown, choose a "Pre-built Demo Photo" (e.g., the iPhone), and click **"Submit Return Request"**.

### Step 6: Instant Approval & Hyperlocal Routing
1. **Action:** The UI shifts to the **Return Status** tab. Watch the "Return Status Tracker" fill up.
2. **Talking Point:** "Our backend uses *Amazon Nova Pro* to visually scan the uploaded photo. You can see the AI mapped the phone and identified the condition flaws in the **AI Condition Scan** panel. Because the AI verified the condition instantly, we issue an **Instant Refund**."
3. **Talking Point:** "Crucially, look at the bottom panel. Because the item was instantly graded as Grade B, we don't ship it to a warehouse. We immediately routed it to a local buyer in the same city. We just saved **52 Kg of CO2 emissions** by keeping the item hyperlocal."

### Step 7: The Business Value (Seller Dashboard)
1. **Action:** Click **"Switch Role"** in the top right, and then select **"I am a Seller"**.
2. **Talking Point:** "Finally, let's look at the business impact. The *Seller Dashboard* provides live telemetry on the impact of our AI. You can see our **Warehouse Avoidance Rate**, the thousands of **Scope-3 Carbon Emissions Avoided**, and that our **GNN Fraud Engine** passively blocked 14 malicious returns this week."

---

### 🎯 Conclusion for Judges
"SecondLife Commerce isn't just an e-commerce store. It is a complete reimagining of the reverse logistics supply chain—using AI to intercept bad purchases, automate condition grading, and route returns hyper-locally to save the planet and the bottom line."
